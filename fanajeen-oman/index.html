Why Fanajeen

A little corner of the coast

Somewhere to slow down with a good coffee and the sea in view — morning, afternoon or an easy evening by the water.

Right on the seafront

Tables with the Azaiba shoreline just steps away — sea breeze included.

Fresh all day

Breakfast into the afternoon, plus bites, sweets and cold drinks whenever.

Coffee done properly

Espresso pulled fresh, milk poured with care — hot or over ice.

Easy to reach

On the Azaiba beach road with parking close by — dine in or take away.
